# DXD AI Capability Centre

## Description
A central website and knowledge hub for the DXD team's AI capability building efforts. It gives internal team members a shared home for resources, projects, and updates — and gives stakeholders a clear, accessible window into what the team does and the value it delivers.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Stakeholders Don't Know What We Do
The DXD AI Capability Centre gives stakeholders a curated, always-current view of the team's mission, active initiatives, and outcomes — making it easy to understand the team's work without scheduling a meeting.

### Problem 2: Resources and Knowledge Are Scattered
Team guides, tools, frameworks, and learnings are spread across emails, drives, and chat threads. The Centre consolidates everything into a single searchable repository that the whole team can rely on.

### Problem 3: Knowledge Gets Lost Over Time
When team members move on, institutional knowledge walks out with them. By documenting projects, learnings, and resources in one place, the Centre preserves the team's collective knowledge and keeps it accessible.

### Problem 4: Onboarding New Team Members Is Slow
New joiners struggle to find context about the team and its work. The Centre gives them a structured starting point — team info, active projects, and curated resources — so they can get up to speed faster.

## Key Features
- Team overview with mission, structure, and member profiles
- Project and initiative tracker with status and outcomes
- Learning resource library (guides, tools, frameworks, training links)
- News and updates feed for announcements and milestones
- Stakeholder-friendly presentation of AI capability building efforts
