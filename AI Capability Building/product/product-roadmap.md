# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. About the Team
Introduces the DXD AI Capability Building team — mission, goals, org structure, and team member profiles — so stakeholders and newcomers understand who we are and what we stand for.

### 2. Projects & Initiatives
Showcases current and past AI projects and initiatives with status, descriptions, and outcomes — giving stakeholders visibility into the team's active work and impact.

### 3. Learning Resources
A curated library of guides, tools, frameworks, and training materials that the team uses and recommends — serving as the go-to repository for AI capability building knowledge.

### 4. News & Updates
A running feed of team announcements, milestones, and progress highlights — keeping stakeholders and team members informed about what's happening.
